# 📊 Business Analytics Dashboard Suite

This repository contains **three interactive Power BI reports** focused on business insights, financial performance, and content analytics. Each report is built using real-world datasets and includes multiple analytical views for decision-making.

---

## 📁 Projects Included

### 1. E-Commerce Sales & Profit Report
- Analyzes **$9M+ sales data** across categories, regions, and customer segments  
- Includes **multiple dashboards** covering sales trends, profit analysis, returns, and shipping insights  
- Highlights **high-sales low-profit products** and key revenue drivers  

---

### 2. Financial Performance Report
- Evaluates **$1.85B income vs $1.88B expenses** with profitability insights  
- Contains **1 focused dashboard tab** for financial KPIs, budget vs actuals, and department analysis  
- Identifies **cost inefficiencies and profit trends**  

---

### 3. Netflix Content Analytics Report
- Analyzes **5800+ titles** across genres, ratings, and production countries  
- Includes **multiple dashboards** covering content distribution, ratings, and growth trends  
- Provides insights into **genre popularity and audience preferences**  

---

## 🛠 Tools & Technologies
- **Power BI** (Data Visualization & Dashboarding)  
- **DAX** (Data Analysis Expressions)  
- **Data Modeling**

 ## 🎯 Purpose of Reports

### 📊 E-Commerce Sales & Profit Report

To **analyze sales, profitability, and customer behavior** across categories and regions in order to **identify revenue drivers, reduce losses, and optimize business performance**.

---

### 💰 Financial Performance Report

To **evaluate financial health by comparing income, expenses, and profitability trends**, enabling **cost control, budget optimization, and strategic decision-making**.

---

### 🎬 Netflix Content Analytics Report
 
To **analyze content distribution, genre performance, and audience ratings** in order to **understand viewer preferences and support data-driven content strategy**.
